Where Creativity Takes Center Stage: join us at ideaUTSAV to

solve REAL-WORLD problems with your unique ideas

HASHTAG , the official tech society of JIMS Engineering Management Technical Campus, invites all of you to IdeaUTSAV, our internal Ideathon .

An Ideathon is a creative marathon where participants come together to brainstorm , collaborate , and transform their innovative ideas into reality. It's like a hackathon, but instead of focusing solely on coding, Ideathon encourages everyone, regardless of their technical background, to share their unique perspectives and ideas to solve real-world problems. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or a non-tech student, Ideathon provides a platform for you to unleash your creativity, learn new skills, and make a positive impact on society. Join us as we embark on this exciting journey of ideation and innovation!

Participants will have to make a team of 2 or 3 members and register as a team .

Teams will be provided with problem statements to choose from and develop their idea based to the chosen problem only.

IdeaUTSAV is scheduled for Thursday, April 18th. Don't delay, assemble your team and register today.
